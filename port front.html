Flames Calculator

It is a fun and lighthearted game often associated with discovering the potential relationship between two individuals. "FLAMES" is an acronym representing different relationship statuses: Friendship, Love, Affection, Marriage, Enmity, and Sibling.
The game typically involves taking the names of two people, removing common letters, and then counting the remaining letters in the combined names to determine the outcome. Each letter corresponds to one of the relationship statuses, and by eliminating letters, the game reveals the relationship status between the individuals.
It's meant for entertainment and amusement, providing a playful way to explore the hypothetical relationship dynamics between two people based on the letters in their names.
